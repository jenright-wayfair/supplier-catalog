Objections & Troubleshooting

Advice
"The chair feels loose"

Explain the "pad-over-chaise" design necessity—loose fabric prevents tearing during motion.

Fix
"The footrest won't close"

Verify user is reaching the 3rd (highest) click before attempting to pull it down.

Advice
"The back is too hard to move"

Show user the tension-adjustment wing nuts located directly beneath the mechanism.

Fix
"Remote is unresponsive"

Check the side-pocket holster; the "safety lock" switch may be accidentally toggled.
